Urban & Rural Sustainability

Smart bicycle security can lead to inceases in bike ownership. We examine ways in which this can also help reduce CO2 emissions both in urban and rural spaces. Data on carbon emission savings is the key for planning and implementation of suitable green mobility strategies.

Bicycle theft is a major obstacle for bicycle ownership. BikeTrace is actively engaged in reducing theft and increasing bike ownership. We firmly believe this creates the perfect opportunity to reinvent urban spaces and make them more liveable.
