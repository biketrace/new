Make your Bike Smarter

Urban Mobility

As we enter a new chapter in Human History there has never been a better time to own a bicycle. BikeTrace smart lock can solve a wide range of challenges relating to bicycle sharing, and bike parking, especially for services such as food delivery and postal distribution where there is a need for short and quick bicycle stops to drop off or collect items.

Making Bicycles Smarter

BikeTrace is transforming bicycles by adding the necessary information capabilities for connectivity within cities. We are also keen on cycling behavioural analysis, as this can help gaining data insights on how to build new biking infrastructure, and scale old ones.

Green Mobility

We examine ways in which this can also help reduce CO2 emissions both in urban and rural spaces. Data on carbon emission savings is the key for planning and implementation of suitable green mobility strategies.

Sustainable Livability

BikeTrace is actively engaged in promoting bicycle ownership. We firmly believe that this creates the perfect opportunity to reinvent urban spaces and make them more sustainable and liveable.
